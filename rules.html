❓ Frequently Asked Questions

💰 When do I receive my tokens?

Every Monday after the week ends (Sunday). Distributions are announced in Telegram.

📊 How do I check my points?

Check the tracking sheet (posted daily in Telegram) or ask a mod. Full transparency!

📸 What if I can't provide proof?

Unfortunately, no proof = no points. Verification is required to maintain fairness and prevent fraud.

🎲 How does the lucky draw work?

Everyone with 10+ points that week is entered. Random winner selected. Even small contributors can win big!

⚖️ Can I appeal if I disagree?

Yes! DM a mod with your case. We'll review and explain our decision transparently.

🔄 Do I have to participate every week?

No! Participate when you want. No pressure. This is about building community, not obligations.
